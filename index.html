SUPER BOWL LX BOUND

The New England Patriots take on the Seattle Seahawks in a historic rematch

Super Bowl LX (60) is scheduled to take place on Sunday, February 8, 2026, at Levi's Stadium in Santa Clara, California. This championship game for the 2025 NFL season features a high-stakes rematch of Super Bowl XLIX between the Seattle Seahawks and the New England Patriots, both of whom finished the regular season with dominant 14â€“3 records. The event is a massive cultural takeover of the San Francisco Bay Area, highlighted by a colorful logo featuring the Golden Gate Bridge and redwood trees, and a highly anticipated Apple Music Halftime Show headlined by global superstar Bad Bunny. Kickoff is set for 6:30 p.m. ET, with the live broadcast on peacock.




Why is this matchup unexpected in the Super Bowl:

  • Drastic Turnarounds: The Patriots went from a 4-13 record last year to becoming one of the most improved teams in NFL history, reaching the Super Bowl under coach Mike Vrabel and quarterback Drake Maye.
  • Long-Shot Odds: Before the 2025 season, the Seahawks had roughly 60-to-1 odds to make the Super Bowl, while the Patriots were considered an even bigger underdog at around 80-to-1.
  • New Eras: Neither team is led by their former dynasty-era superstars. Instead, the game features Sam Darnold leading the Seahawks and Drake Maye for the Patriots.
  • Rematch Context: While this is a rematch of the famous 2015 Super Bowl XLIX, it is a surprise because neither team was projected to be here after missing the postseason last year.
